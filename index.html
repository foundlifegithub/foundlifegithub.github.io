What We Do?

We are enhancing the emergency services by using the Technoligies such as IOT, Smart communications  and real time tracking of vehicle.

Indian roads are over occupied and highly dense. Most of the people are not literate enough to follow basic driving courtesy. This creates troublesome situation for emergency vehicles and other good samaritans too. We aim to provide clear crossroads to the emergency vehicles at traffic signals. One of the concerns for us is, manipulating the sequence of access to traffic on crossroads, without disturbing the default flow. We aim to use the best of the technological advancements to achieve the aim.
Foundlife is the best solution for this situations,
we are working towards enhancment of these services by our own developed Algorithm.
We give Clear path to every emergency vehicle without breaking the basic rules of the traffic.
  1. Real time Monitoring - We track each and every vehicle through our dedicated tracking device preinstalled on the vehicle itself.
  2. Centralized Decision - The server does calculations of ETA based on the data provided by the device and sends the message to the respective signal.
  3. Clear Path - ch The Signal handles rest of the procedure , it gives green signal to the ambulance without breaking the sequance of the signal, i.e. if the Ambulance is arriving from lane 4 and current green signal is for lane 2 then it will first give green signal to lane 3 and then lane 4 will get green signal.
